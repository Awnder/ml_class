Visualize storms like never before

Meet Storm Radar, the all-in-one, high-resolution storm and hurricane tracker app for iOS, designed to help you stay safe and informed when severe weather approaches.

Advanced Map Layers

Customize your interactive radar map with a variety of detailed overlays including wind, temperature, lightning, tropical and winter storms, and more.

Severe Weather Alerts

Get notified of incoming rain with real-time precipitation alerts for your area. Get live local storm alerts including NOAA/National Weather Service severe weather watches, warnings and advisories.

6-Hour Future Radar

Know exactly when and where the storm will hit with 6 hours of global future radar.

Storm Tracks

Get a detailed view of the changing weather patterns in your area in vivid color, including how much wind and rain are coming your way. Understand a storms path via motion vectors and up to 30 advanced weather features.
