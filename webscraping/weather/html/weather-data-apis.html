Flexible Weather Data APIs: From essential to enterprise

Access diverse weather intelligence through our Weather Data APIs – from basic conditions and almanac data to high-resolution radar imagery and personal weather station feeds – all customizable to your industry’s specific needs.

Standard Weather APIs

For broad applications requiring reliable and straightforward weather intelligence, Standard Weather APIs deliver foundational weather data in 4 km resolution.

  • Real-time weather updates and current conditions
  • 15-day weather forecasts with hourly precision
  • Global severe weather alerts*
  • High-resolution weather imagery
  • Historical weather data access

*Available for US, Canada, Western Europe, Japan, and Australia

Premium Weather APIs

Premium Weather APIs provide complex industries with advanced insights, including past weather details for trend analysis, training analytical models, and critical business decisioning.

  • Advanced probabilistic weather forecasts covering a full range of possible outcomes
  • Seasonal and sub-seasonal predictions
  • Industry-specific endpoints such as aviation, energy, agriculture, and financial services
  • High-resolution gridded data
  • Third-party integrations
  • Weather analytics and insights API

FAQs

What are Weather Data APIs?

Weather Data APIs provide on-demand, real-time access to global weather data, including current conditions, forecasts, alerts, and historical information. We also offer specialized data for unique use cases, such as agriculture, aviation, and energy. Contact us today for an API key.

What are Standard Weather APIs?

Standard Weather APIs provide essential weather data for a wide range of use cases, offering core weather insights such as current conditions, daily and hourly forecasts, weather imagery, and basic historical weather data. These APIs are ideal for businesses needing standard weather data for general operational use or mass consumption applications.

Features include:

  • Global 4 km resolution for current and forecasted weather conditions.
  • Access to essential data: 15-day daily and hourly forecasts, government weather alerts, radar imagery, and air quality.
  • Historical weather records, including daily and hourly summaries.
  • Consumer-focused lifestyle indices, such as pollen, flu, driving difficulty, and frost potential.

What are Premium Weather APIs?

Premium Weather APIs offer advanced weather capabilities for specialized use cases, including high-resolution geospatial data, probabilistic forecasting, seasonal and sub-seasonal forecasts, and industry-specific applications. These APIs cater to enterprises requiring precise, customizable weather intelligence for critical operations and decision-making.

Features include:

  • Gridded and polygonal forecast data at high resolutions, enabling detailed spatial analysis.
  • Probabilistic forecasting and insights into the likelihood of weather events ideal for risk management and optimization.
  • Industry-focused solutions, such as renewable energy forecasts (solar irradiance, wind speed), agriculture (soil moisture, crop-specific evapotranspiration), and severe weather tracking (hail, lightning, tornadoes).
  • Enhanced historical data with extended coverage and granularity.
  • Advanced API capabilities for integrating into enterprise tools like ESRI or internal systems for efficient data management and decision support.

Can your system handle large-scale data requests?

Our platform is built for enterprise-scale performance and reliability, handling billions of requests daily with low latency. Powered by globally distributed cloud infrastructure, our forecast engine APIs deliver high availability and scalability, ensuring seamless access even at high request volumes.

Can I customize the weather data output?

Absolutely. To receive only the necessary information, you can specify which data parameters, such as temperature, wind speed, or precipitation, to include in your API response.

What should I do if my API key isn’t working?

If your API key isn’t working, follow these steps:

  • Verify that your API key is correctly entered.
  • Check if you’ve exceeded your usage limits or if your plan has expired.
  • If issues persist, please contact our support team for assistance.
