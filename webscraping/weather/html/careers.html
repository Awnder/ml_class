Careers

Join a passionate, purpose-driven team that thrives on using science, data, and expertise to drive faster, better decisions for the millions of consumers, clients, and partners who rely on us every day.

Do you run towards problems?

Great, you’re in the right place. Every single person is impacted by weather, and given our increasingly volatile climate, there are no shortage of problems to solve. Our team is driven by curiosity, committed to selfless innovation, and fueled by making a difference. Every day. We take great pride in being the world’s most accurate forecaster1 and a top 10 most trusted brand2.

What’s our secret?

Our people; they’re the heart of who we are and what we do.

There’s a reason nearly half of our employees have spent more than a decade growing their careers at The Weather Company. And the influx of new talent is what makes us even stronger, more diverse, and more inclusive.

Helping you thrive

We are proud to offer a comprehensive benefits package that not only attracts top talent but also fosters a culture of well-being and growth.

Health

Variety of medical insurance options including a $0-cost, premium employee coverage benefit

Financial

Competitive 401K match with no vesting requirement, national health, dental, and vision plans

Home office

Generous home office reimbursements for all employees (hybrid and remote)

Family

Progressive family plan benefits

Flexibility

Remote work options/flexibility

Development

Ongoing learning and development programs
